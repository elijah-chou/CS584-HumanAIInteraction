Legion: Writing Assistant Daemons

How to Use Legion

Hi there! Legion is a simple text editor that also has a few built-in "Daemons" to give you immediate feedback on your writing. These Daemons are designed to help you improve your writing by giving you suggestions on how to improve your writing. You can also use Legion to write your own stories, essays, or whatever you want! To get feedback, simply click on one of three different Daemon buttons in the editor toolbar to get feedback! The feedback will be given to you above your own writing. Have fun writing!

If it's your first time using this, please read the daemon descriptions below to better understand what each daemon has to offer.

If you want to jump into writing, click here to navigate directly to the text editor.

Helpful Assistant

A daemon that will review all your writing and give you feedback depending on your chosen parameters. For this daemon, you can have your entire writing reviewed by clicking the button directly. It may sometimes give a suggestion as to how to rewrite your writing! A brief description of each parameter is given below.

  • Length of Feedback: option to change the number of tips you receive from the daemon
  • Approach: option to get feedback that is more likely to apply holistically to your writing and not necessarily to a specific part of your writing, or to get feedback that is more likely refer to specific phrases or sentences in your writing
  • Formality: option to potentially get tips on making your writing more casual or formal

Devil's Advocate

A daemon that will review your writing as a Devil's Advocated and give you feedback on specific sentence highlighted in red. A brief description of each parameter is given below.

  • Depth of Analysis: option to get a more shallow or in-depth analysis of your writing
  • Focus: option to choose what aspect of writing the daemon should specifically address in it's feedback
  • Tone of Feedback: option to get a friendly, neutral, or aggressive tone in the daemon's analysis

Creative Mastermind

A daemon that takes all your writing and rewrites it in a more creative fashion based on your chosen parameters. Brief description of each parameter is given below. **NOTE**: This daemon will add more figurative language, humor, and advanced vocabulary to your writing. It will also tend to expand on your original content, so be sure to read through the new suggestions carefully if you need to ensure that information accuracy is maintained!

  • Humor: option to add more/reduce humor to the daemon's generated suggestion
  • Figurative Language: option to add more/reduce figurative language to the daemon's generated suggestion
  • Vocabulary: option to use simple/intermediate/advanced vocabulary to the daemon's generated suggestion
